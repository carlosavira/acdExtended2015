Blue Warriors Bag Championship in 4th CEAP Mindanao Games

MAY 23, 2014

ACD’s High School Boys Basketball Team dominated the hardcourt to emerge as Champions in the 4th Catholic Educational Association of the Philippines (CEAP) Mindanao Games held Friday, January 30 at the UIC Bajada Gym here in the city.

The team was Region XI’s representative to the Games in the Secondary division while St. John Paul II College of Davao was the region’s representative in the collegiate division.

Under the mentorshipof Assistant Coach Tristan Balino, the ACD Blue Warriorsrallied to a strong head startin the final game, leading from quarter to quarteragainst Region IX represented by St. Columban College of Pagadian City, and finishing to an 89-68 victory.

Davao Association of Catholic Schools (DACS) Executive Director Mr. Jimmie-Lou dela Vegaawarded the trophy and medals to the Champions together with overall event coordinator, Mr. Billy Cruzada, and Basketball Tournament Manager,Mr. Leonardo Clarin.

This year’s CEAP Mindanao Gameswas participated by six hundred athletes and coaches from Region IX, Region X, Region XI, and Region XIII, that competed in nine different sporting events including Basketball, Volleyball, Chess, Dancesport, Table Tennis, Taekwondo, Swimming, Badminton, and SepakTakraw.
