NEW STUDENTS (First Year)


Requirements:

  1. Notice of Application Result (Qualified for Admission)
  2. Report Card (original copy)
  3. Birth Certificate from NSO (1 photocopy and 1 original copy)
  4. Birth Certificate from NSO (1 photocopy and 1 original copy)
  5. Certificate of Good Moral Character (from previous school – original copy)
  6. Passport-size ID picture with name-tag (can be done inside the school campus at a reasonable price – 3 copies)

PROCEDURE: (Please follow the steps below.)

  • STEP 1 – Covered Court
    • Fill-in official registration form and one (1) ID slip completely, clearly/legibly and have it checked and signed by the Enrolment in-Charge. (You may ask assistance from the working students assigned in the registration for your schedule of classes.)

  • STEP 2– Records and Admissions Office (RAO)
    • Submit the official registration form with all the requirements to Window 2 for encoding of subjects enrolled and wait for the copy of the class schedule and ID slip to be given to you.

  • STEP 3 – Finance/Cashiers’ Office
    • Submit copy of the class schedule and pay enrolment fees to either Window 6 or Window 7 and wait for the official receipt, student’s copy of class schedule and ID slip stamped as “ENROLLED” to be given to you.

  • STEP 4 – ID Center (Ground Floor > next to HS Guidance Office)
    • Present official receipt and ID slip stamped as “ENROLLED” to secure school ID.

  • STEP 5 – HRD Office (Ground Floor > next to Finance/Cashiers’ Office)
    • Present the school ID with the official receipt to Window 10 for validation.
