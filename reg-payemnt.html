  2. Other Mode of Payment

    Metrobankdirect (Internet)
    • STEP 1: Login to your metrobank account and select Pay Bills

      For personal accounts, metrobankdirect URL is https://personal .metrobankdirect.com.,
      For corporate accounts, metrobankdirect URL is https://corporate.metrobankdirect.com

    • STEP 2: Under SPECIAL BILL, choose ASSUMPTION COLLLEGE OF DAVAO from the dropdown list of billers.

    • STEP 3: Enter the STUDENT NUMBER (8-digits) under Subscribers/Account no.

    • STEP 4: Choose an account where the tuition fee payment will be sourced from and enter the amount you wished to pay. Select payment type and click on CONTINUE.

      A page acknowledging your transaction will appear. Click PRINT for a copy of this page. A transaction acknowledgement will also be sent to your provided email address. If you are not yet enrolled to metrobankdirect, you may enroll at your Metrobank depository branch.

    ATM
    • STEP 1: Insert your ATM card and enter your personal identification number.
    • STEP 2: On the Metrobank ATM screen, select BILLS PAYMENT.
    • STEP 3: Select OTHERS and then ASSUMPTION COLLEGE OF DAVAO.
    • STEP 4: Input the STUDENT NUMBER (8-digits)
    • STEP 5: Get the transaction receipt. This serves as your proof payment.
    Over-the-Counter Branch tellers
    • STEP 1: Go to the nearest or most convenient Metrobank branch and get a copy of the Payment Slip (green form) and accomplish the required information/details:

      • Company Name: ASSUMPTION COLLEGE OF DAVAO 828 (Utility Code)
      • Subscriber Name: STUDENT’S NAME (Last Name, First Name, Middle Initial)
      • Subscriber Number: STUDENT NUMBER (8-digits)
      • Mode of Payment: CASH/CHECK/DEBIT ACCOUNT Write the AMOUNT OF PAYMENT

      • (Note: Slips are to be accomplished in duplicate copies.) 1st Copy-Bank copy; 2nd Copy-Client’s copy


    • STEP 2: Present payment slip to Metrobank tellers together with
    • STEP 3: Cash/Check.
    • STEP 4: Once validated, the teller will give you your validated copy. Before leaving, check and verify the copy received if validated and if the copy was the one accomplished and presented to the bank teller.

    The payment slip will serve as the proof of payment.
