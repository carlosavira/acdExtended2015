Blue Warriors Bag Championship in 4th CEAP Mindanao Games

ACD’s High School Boys Basketball Team dominated the hardcourt to emerge as Champions in the 4th Catholic Educational Association of the Philippines (CEAP) Mindanao Games held Friday, January 30 at the UIC Bajada Gym here in the city. The team was Region XI’s representative to the Games in the Secondary division while St. John Paul II College of Davao was the region’s representative in the collegiate division.
