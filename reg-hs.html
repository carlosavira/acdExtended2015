REQUIREMENTS:

Old Pupils/Students:

  • Report Card

New Pupils/Students:

  • Notice of Application Result (Qualified)
  • Report Card (Form 138) - original
  • Birth Certificate (if possible NSO copy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (high school only)
  • ESC Grantee Certificate (for ESC Grantees from previous school)
  • National Achievement Test (NAT) - For 1ST Year enrollees only
  1. Please don’t forget to write the general average of the pupil/student on the upper right corner of the Registration Form.
  2. Look for the name of old pupil/student in the list provided and write the date enrolled on the space provided. For the new pupil/student, write the name and the date enrolled on the space provided below the list of old pupils/students.
  3. Let the parent/student accomplish the Registration Form completely, accurately and legibly.
  4. Collect the accomplished Form. Take time to see if:
    1. all information asked are written;
    2. the parent and the pupil/student has signed the “Statement of Commitment”.
  5. Sign the form if all items are accomplished. Don’t forget to indicate the date registered after your signature.
  6. Secure and fill-in one copy of book slip. (Covered Court)
  7. Parent/Student may then proceed to either Window 3 or Window 4 for encoding. (RAO Office)
  8. After encoding, present the Registration Form to either Window 7 or Window 8 for payment of Miscellaneous, Tuition, Books, and PTA membership fees (Cashier’s Office) REGISTRATION FORMS MUST BE SUBMITTED TO THE CASHIER UPON PAYMENT.
  9. Parent/s together with the pupils/students may then proceed to HS AVR for picture-taking in order to secure a school ID.
  10. Finally, parents/students may now claim the books. (Present official receipt and book slip.) (TEXTBOOK CENTER)
